This innovative app leverages the power of technology to facilitate seamless communication through American Sign Language (ASL).

ASL Translator serves as a bridge between the hearing-impaired and those unfamiliar with American Sign Language. It empowers individuals to express themselves effectively, fostering a more inclusive and accessible communication environment.
